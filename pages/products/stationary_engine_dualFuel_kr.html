  1. Two-Stage T/C System
    High efficiency and no derating Even for sites with high ambient temperature and altitude Extreme miller cycle, Two-stage T/C
    • Advanced IVC
    • Effective compression ratio
    • Higher Engine efficiency
    • Decreased NOx emission
  2. Control System
    Safe and optimal engine operation HI-MECS, Cylinder balancing, knock control
  3. Combustion System
    High power and efficiency Pre-chamber, Spark plug/Ignition coil, Piston bowl
    • Output / Cylinder : 1400kWm
    • Engine Cycle : 4:stroke
    • Bore : 540/600mm
    • Engine Speed : 600rpm
  4. Gas Supply System
    Even mixture distribution & Low knocking Gas mixer optimization, Port flow CFD
  5. Intake/Exhaust System
    Low load performance and automatic NOx control Dual Valve timing, Exhaust waste gate

  • 안정적인 성능 엔진의 주요 요소 중 하나는 엔진의 성능 일관성입니다. HiMSEN 엔진의 전문 엔지니어링은 수년 후에도 안정적인 출력을 보장할 수 있습니다.
  • 손쉬운 유지 관리 현대중공업의 엔진은 유지보수가 용이하도록 신중하게 모듈화되어 있습니다. 현대중공업 발전소에서 일하고 있는 많은 O&M 매니저들은 직관적이고 안정적인 엔진 디자인이 현장 운영을 더 쉽게 해준다고 말합니다. 또한 파이프리스 디자인은 변형을 방지할 수 있습니다.

Termonorte / 93MW DFPP
The biggest dual fuel engine power plant in Colombia

In February 2017, HYUNDAI received an order from TERMONORTE S.A.S E.S.P., for engineering, procurement and construction. The contract consists of 10 sets of HiMSEN dual fuel engine generator to supply continuous power to national grid in Colombia, South America. The power plant was handed over in November 2018 to the customer and is currently under commercial operation.
